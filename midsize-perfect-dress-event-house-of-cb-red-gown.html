    A FASHIONISTA has raved about the selection of House of CB dresses available to flatter her midsize figure.

    She visited Nordstrom in search of an elegant dress to wear for an upcoming posh event.

    Faith Hill (@faithmonethill) boasts over 31,000 followers on TikTok, where she shares beauty and fashion buys.

    She took to the social media platform to try on dresses that she had picked up during a shopping spree at House of CB.

    The Texas-based influencer looked in good spirits as she styled her hair out of the way in a bun and kept her makeup minimal.

    “Try on some House of CB dresses with me,” she said while standing in the changing room.

    “I need a dress for an event and I went to House of CB because they always hit.”

    CORSETED RED GOWN 

    Faith started her search for the perfect dress by trying on the brand’s Stefania satin gown.

    The corseted gown is available for $285 on the Nordstrom website in sizes ranging from XS to L.

    Happy customers have given the floor-length frock a perfect five-star rating, however Faith was left unimpressed by the fit after trying it in a medium.

    “The first one I tried on was this red one that wouldn’t zip in the back,” she said.

    “And then the large was too big so we didn't get it but I liked her.”

    IVORY MINI DRESS

    Faith changed into House of CB’s Adriana lace inset satin mini dress in a size medium, which cost $239.

    The stylish dress is sold in three different colors that the brand claims are “sure to turn heads on your night out.”

    Previous shoppers have given it a four-star rating and penned reviews warning that the fit runs slightly small.

    “I tried on this one just for fun but I feel like I need this in my life,” Faith said. 

    “It’s literally perfect, so girly and dainty.”

    FLORAL COCKTAIL DRESS

    The style enthusiast continued her shopping haul by trying on a curve-hugging cocktail dress for $255.

    It is sold in sizes ranging from XS to XL with the option to buy a fuller cup version.

    “Next is this beautiful dress that if it fit the vibe I was going for more, I would’ve gotten her,” Faith said.

    “It’s a medium and she’s just gorgeous. She fits perfectly, girly, dainty, all the things.”

    PURPLE FROCK

    She finished her visit to Nordstrom by trying on House of CB’s Azia floral underwire cocktail dress.

    The $255 dress is stocked in dark purple, cream print, and flame orange with sizes ranging from XS to XL.

    “Then I tried on this purple dress in an extra large just for fun,” Faith said.

    Despite the dress seemingly sculpting her figure perfectly she didn’t go ahead with getting it for the event. 

    She teased viewers that they would have to continue following her page to see a video of the little black dress that she decided to buy.

    The video went viral with over 66,000 views and thousands of likes from people who were amazed by the outfits.

    Many people took to the comment section sharing their opinions and encouraging Faith to buy all of the dresses.

    “The second one was made for you OMG,” one person wrote.

    “I love them all [heart eyes] You’re a dream! I love you,” another said.

    “You’ve convinced me to go. I’ve been eyeing the purple,” a third chimed in.
