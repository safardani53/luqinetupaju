    Nonito Donaire Parents: Nonito Donaire Sr And Imelda Donaire

    Nonito Donaire Jr. was to Nonito C. Donaire, Sr. and Imelda M. Gonzales November 16, 1982 in Talibon, Bohol, Philippines.

    His father, Nonito Donaire Sr., is a former boxer who competed in the flyweight division.

    Moreover, he is a two-time Philippine national champion and a silver medalist at the 1988 Asian Games.

    Imelda Donaire is not into sports and has always supported her kids. They raised him in Talibon, where he was the third of their four children.
