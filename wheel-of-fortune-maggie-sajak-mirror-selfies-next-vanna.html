    The daughter of iconic host Pat Sajak playfully looked back on 2023, treating followers to her most alluring mirror selfies of the year.

    Maggie, 28, manages Wheel of Fortune's online presence and graces the screen during commercial breaks, leaving a lasting imprint on game show aficionados.

    Many fans are convinced that Maggie will inherit Vanna White's coveted puzzle board role in the future. 

    Her Instagram drop on Monday offered a tantalizing peek into the glamorous looks she might sport in the coveted position and others that were simply casually sexy. 

    In one snapshot, Maggie rocked a barely-there white top paired with a lace bra, exuding confidence and allure.
