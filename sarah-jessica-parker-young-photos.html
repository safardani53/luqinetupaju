    Take a look back at Sarah Jessica Parker throughout her incredible career in Hollywood, here.

    Sarah, a true icon, and actress known for famed shows and movies like ‘Hocus Pocus’, ‘Failure to Launch’, ‘Divorce’, and, of course, ‘Sex and the City,’ turns 58, today, March 25. SATC was what ultimately made SJP a household name, when it premiered in 1999. As Carrie Bradshaw, she played a newspaper columnist with a seemingly unlimited clothes budget, relationship drama, and three best friends. It ran until 2004, and spawned two movies.

    Sarah was in a relationship with Robert Downey Jr. from 1984 to 1991; they ultimately split as he battled drug addiction. One of her brothers introduced her to her now-husband, Matthew Broderick, at a comedy club in 1997. They married that year, and have three kids together: son James, and twin daughters Marion and Tabitha.
