    How much is Stuart Broad worth?

    Stuart Broad's net worth is estimated to be around £53.7million ($69million).

    The fast bowler has made his money from his cricket career, as well as from a pub called Tap & Run, which is located near Nottingham, and sponsorship deals.

    Stuart has joined with brands such as Adidas, Red Bull, Hardys, Simon and Schuster, The Belfry, and Investec.

    How much do England cricket players get paid?

    An England cricket player can earn between £650,000 to £1million per year.

    The ECB hands out three different kind of contracts.

    These contract types are called central contract, increment, and pace bowling development.

    Central contracts - which Broad received in 2023 - are given out to players who play regularly and can be worth up to £900,000.

    Increment contracts are awarded to players who are playing in the international arena, which means that they get an additional salary.

    And pace bowling development contracts are given to the best young bowlers who will be given more ECB training ahead of an England call-up.
