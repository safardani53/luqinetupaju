    ULTA announced that it is changing its store hours the day before the biggest shopping event of the year.

    The beauty store has already launched some online-only Black Friday deals, but there are more savings to come.

    However, bargain hunters will have to wait until November 24 to get in-store deals as Ulta will be closed on Thanksgiving.

    Luckily, stores will open on Black Friday at 6 am, as confirmed to The U.S. Sun by an Ulta employee.

    There are over 150 deals available through November 14, including $10, $15, or $20 off each purchase - even on prestige brands and fragrances.

    Shoppers can also get a free 22-piece beauty bag with a purchase of $90.

    read more on retail

    There are four bags to choose from that feature brands like Urban Decay, Tula, Yves Saint Laurent, and Kylie Cosmetics.

    Other sales include 40% off hair tools and brushes, 40% off styling products, and 40% off eye products.

    NEW PRODUCTS FOR THE HOLIDAYS

    Just ahead of the holiday season, Ulta introduced Lush items exclusively online.

    Lush is a worldwide favorite that sells unique bath bombs and a wide range of skincare products.

    Most read in Money

    Previous to its partnership with Ulta, shoppers could only get Lush items at its own stores and through Asos in the UK.

    Aside from a brief stint at Macy's in the early 2000s, Lush has had "very limited third-party retail partnerships," reports Retail Dive.

    “As wellness and self-care continue to be a priority at Ulta Beauty, we’re excited to evolve our assortment with brands like Lush who share our passion for this space,” Penny Coy, Ulta’s vice president of merchandising, said in a statement.

    “As pioneers in the body category, we are delighted to provide greater access to their beloved products, and together help amplify their mission for efficacious, fresh, and cruelty-free ingredients.”

    On Ulta's website, shoppers can find some of Lush's best-selling products and holiday sets.

    The products will range in price from $17 to $46.

    The beauty store made another big change with the launch of The Wellness Shop.
