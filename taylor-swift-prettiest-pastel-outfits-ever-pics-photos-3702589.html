    Taylor Swift really made pastels the color of spring and summer when she made the beautiful, soft hues the color scheme and aesthetic for her 2019 album Lover. Now that we’re finally in the full swing of spring, we are reminiscing on some of our favorite pastel looks from the Grammy winning star. Let’s take a look at some of Taylor’s very best pastel looks through the years!

    Taylor Swift At The 2019 Billboard Music Awards

    At the 2019 Billboard Music Awards, Taylor hit the red carpet in a lavender mini dress dress by Raisa & Vanessa. At this point, she hadn’t even announced her new album, but was already hinting that soft, fun colors would be part of the theme. This dress was another nod to that. The mini featured sheer sleeves and a ruffled design. She also sported earrings by Stefere Jewelry and Graziela Gem, with other jewels by Stephen Webster, Moritz Glik, and Jenny Dee Jewelry, and sandal heels by Casadei. Taylor completed the look with matching makeup. She wore a light pink lip color and her hair pulled back into an updo.

    Taylor Swift At The 2019 TIME 100 Gala

    The singer attended the TIME 100 Gala on April 23, 2019, in a gorgeous pink and yellow gown from J Mendel. The gown was so whimsical and made Taylor look like a radiant princess straight out of a fairytale. The hues were perfect for the spring occasion. Taylor kept the theme going with a pair of lemon-yellow Chloe Gosselin heels and jewelry by Lorraine Schwartz.

    Taylor Swift’s 2019 ‘GMA’ Performance

    Now here’s a fun, flirty look we’d love to recreate this season! While Taylor performed during her April 22, 2019, appearance on Good Morning America, fans couldn’t take their eyes off of her bold fashion choices. She wore a sheer pink, long sleeve, button down top with a black tank underneath, and a pair of high-waisted, sparkling short shorts with hues of purple, pink, blue, and more! Taylor completed the fun number with a pair of black booties with a small heel. Her look radiated the best hues of spring, and she looked so cute set against the carnations just behind her as she performed on stage!

    Taylor Swift At iHeartRadio’s Wango Tango 2019

    This outfit was definitely one of Taylor’s boldest. The singer sported a custom ensemble by Jessica Jones and Joseph Cassell that featured nearly every color of the rainbow during the June 2019 iHeartRadio Wango Tango event! Taylor rocked the jacket with long fringe and even complimented the rest of the look with matching Stella McCartney sneakers. There are plenty more amazing pastel looks from Taylor where these came from!
