    How much does postage from Nike cost?

    Standard delivery from Nike costs £4.50 and takes 2-4 business days, but if you spend over £50 it's free.

    If you want your items the next day it'll cost you £8 and you have to order before 12pm from Monday to Thursday.

    And if you're a NikePlus member you get free standard delivery on every order.

    The retailer also does free returns as long as you send your items back within 60 days.
