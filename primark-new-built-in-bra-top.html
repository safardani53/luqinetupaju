    PRIMARK shoppers are racing to get their hands on the brand new built-in bra top.

    So if you are someone who hates wearing a bra but wants a top that is supportive, you’ll need to listen up. 

    The new vests come with a stitched-in bra, so you’ll be able to go bra-free, without compromising on support.

    Primark took to social media to show off the new top, leaving fashion fans scrambling to nab themselves one.

    Holding up the black top, Primark said: “She’s popular.”

    The chain later added: “In love with our built-in bra top.”

    The vest top is priced at just £10 and is super versatile - while it’s perfect for summer days out, it’s also great for lounging around the house.

    It can be dressed up or dressed down too - so whether you want to pair it with leggings and trainers for running errands, or a sassy skirt and heels for a night out with the girls, this top is bound to suit whatever occasion. 

    At the time of writing, the clip was shared on TikTok, on the official @primark account, just 21 hours prior and it had quickly amassed 399,800 views. 

    Social media users were stunned at the top and many were desperate to get their hands on one.
