With the nickname "Iron Man", he holds the National Basketball Association's record for most consecutive regular matches played during the season with 1,192. Green has played for the Los Angeles Lakers, Phoenix Suns, Dallas Mavericks and the Miami Heat. He's most successful with the Lakers, for whom he won three championships in 1987, 1988 and 2000, and was named NBA All-Star in 1990.
