    James Ronald Ryun is the full name of one of the American former politicians and Olympic track and field athletes. He is widely known by the name ‘Jim Ryun.’ In the 1968 Summer Olympics, he won a silver medal in the 1500 meters and became the first athlete to run from a high school a mile within four minutes.

    Moreover, he is the last American to hold the world record in the mile run and later worked in the United States House of Representatives for 11 years. At that time, he represented Kansas’ 2nd congressional district for the Republican Party.
