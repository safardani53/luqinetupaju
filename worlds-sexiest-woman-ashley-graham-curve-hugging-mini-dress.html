    The post included a short video that captured Ashley striking several modeling poses as she handled construction equipment while filming on the Barbie-themed set.

    Aqua's Barbie Girl played as the HGTV host showed off her famous curves in a skintight, bright-orange cashmere dress which she paired with glittery pink, open-toed heels.

    GORGEOUS IN GREEN

    Last week, Ashley showed off her incredible curves in a tight green dress for her Live with Kelly and Mark appearance.

    Ashley previewed the dress on the Live! Instagram Stories before meeting with Mark Consuelos, 52, and fill-in host Andy Cohen, 55.

    The TV personality revealed that her curve-hugging, sheer green dress was designed by Hildur Yeoman and that she wore a "little bodysuit underneath because it's morning TV."

    The star gave the camera a spin, showing off the floral print details on the dress while also flaunting her curvaceous figure.

    Ashley then struck a number of flirty poses to showcase her outfit, hair—which was styled into a long ponytail—and makeup for her morning talk show appearance.

    That same week, Ashley flaunted her curvaceous figure in another dress as she revealed her Barbie-lookalike doll to fans during a new HGTV clip.

    The Nebraska native showed off her Barbie lookalike doll in the video, which was originally created in 2016.

    The mother-of-two wore a colorful long-sleeved bodycon dress, paired with a tan fuzzy hat.

    Ashley flaunted full glam makeup and wore her long dark hair styled in big loose curls.

    She held the official Ashley Graham Barbie, which featured a wider hip structure and a belly pooch to simulate the model.

    Ashley told cameras: "Never would I have imagined that I had a Barbie made in my likeness when I was a little girl. Never, never, never.

    "I never even thought that Barbie would be able to look different than she did."

    "So the fact that Mattel has done such a great job in creating all different types of Barbies that are made in so many different likenesses just goes to show that any little girl can look up and say 'oh, I can be her too,'" she gushed.

    Ashley then joked that she and her Barbie doll can speak telepathically, as she pretended to have a conversation with miniature Ashley.

    The model continued: "She was created in 2016. I got to go to the Mattel headquarters and actually pick out her hair, the shape of her eyes, of her lips, her nose.

    "She even has wider hips than the OG Barbie and she has a little lower belly fat. We discussed the whole outfit. We took it from one of the red carpets that I did.

    "I love her, I adore her, and she lives in my office actually. And she has a little Glamour of the Year award too," she giggled.

    WARDROBE MALFUNCTION

    Ashley showed off her curves yet again in a black sheer dress while attending the Barbie movie premiere.

    She wowed in the tulle polka-dot gown at the event in Los Angeles on Sunday—but admitted to suffering what she called an awkward AF wardrobe malfunction.

    Ashley wore a strapless dress with a fishtail skirt and heavily ruffled bodice.

    The Countdown to the Oscars host teamed the skintight garment with a pair of towering black heels.

    She looked stunning in her eye-catching outfit, she admitted that she could barely sit down in it.

    Ashley shared a video of herself lying awkwardly to the side in the backseat of a car on the way to the premiere.

    She said: "I'm on my way to the Barbie premiere and this is awkward AF because I can't move."

    Ashley added through gritted teeth: "I'm taped in, I'm sewed in," before declaring: "But I look amazing!"

    Ashley later shared a clip from inside the movie theater of herself eating a Snickers chocolate bar.

    "Movie theater Snickers is the way, the truth, and the light," she said in between bites.
