    Dec. 18 2023, Published 8:23 a.m. ET

    The Gist:

    • Charles Leclerc began dating Alexandra Saint Mleux just months after a breakup.
    • She blew up on social media.
    • Fans are put off by her due to some controversial decisions.

    Racecar driver Charles Marc Hervé Perceval Leclerc has quite the extra special record. Launching his career in 2005, the Formula 1 star started in just karting, winning the French PACA Championship. Since then, Charles has dominated the race track, consistently finding immense success in competitions.

    Charles's personal life is filled with charity and philanthropy, making him the type of guy you would want to take home to mom. The question is: has someone already claimed that spot? Keep reading for all of the details on his dating life.

    Is Charles Lecerc dating anyone?

    While they kept their relationship low-key for quite some time, Charles officially went public with Alexandra Saint Mleux. After all, when they supposedly got together, Charles had just gotten out of a three-year relationship.

    It wouldn't be the best look for him to hard launch someone new right away. Eventually, he was able to publicly introduce the world to his girlfriend which truly put her on the map. Alexandra started gaining social media followers based on her relationship status with one of the world's biggest racers.

    Who is Alexandra Saint Mleux?

    Outside of her love life, Essentially Sports reports that Alexandra is studying Art History in Paris, France. As previously mentioned, her social media following took over, especially on TikTok where she has engaged with fans effectively.

    After gaining a sizable platform, she came under fire for a controversial move. The social media star liked a tweet which "bore a message that many perceived to be tinted with racial undertones, a grim reminder of the invisible barriers that still exist within our society," according to Essentially Sports.

    Some fans were simply not happy about the implications of Alexandra's liked tweet, so they spoke out on social media. In response, a fan account wrote, "[I] got to talk to Alexandra and that was really lovely and I was really happy and 100 percent excited about this, but seeing the liked tweet just made me a bit upset... I'm mixed, too, and wouldn't like to make a page about this kind of person."

    Alexandra stirred up fans' anger again in October 2023. When she showed up to support her boyfriend's race, she wore a pretty red dress. Fans, though, were worried that this was totally bad luck. One wrote: "Just as you thought we were finally happy to see those red pants never again his girlfriend started to wear red, there goes his podium.”

    "This is the reason Charles will never be able to win any world championship because the cheap women who always follow him will make him unlucky," wrote another. Alongside all the hate, Alexandra appeared to make her social media profiles private.

    Regardless of what the fans think about her, Charles and Alexandra continue to be in a happy relationship and he seems totally unbothered by the harsh comments. Charles has taken his girlfriend on lavish vacations and shown her tons of affection.

    In August 2023, they were seen in Italy, sharing some kisses. They seemed to be buzzing with romance as they hung out on the top deck of a yacht, according to Just Jared.
