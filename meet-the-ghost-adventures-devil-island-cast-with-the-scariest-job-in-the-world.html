    Spine-chilling discoveries are in store for Zak and his team members during the Devil Island special. He and his crew investigate the paranormal activity on an island in San Francisco Bay. Deceptively named Angel Island, the Ghost Adventures team uncovers some supernatural mysteries during the episode.

    Zak’s team of paranormal investigators

    Since 2008, Zak and his crew have been investigating paranormal activity in locations all over the world.

    Zak and Aaron have all been on the team since season 1. Jay and Billy became a part of the crew in 2009. Now, they’re all back for season 25.

    Judging by the Devil Island episode, despite doing their job for years, it hasn’t gotten any less terrifying.

    During the two-hour special’s trailer, a member of the team says: “This is the scariest job in the world,” before heading through a doorway.

    Where is Ghost Adventures: Devil Island filmed?

    Ghost Adventures season 25 sees the team head to locations all over America.

    Nevada, Utah and California are just some of the places the show will go in the 2023 season.

    However, Ghost Adventures: Devil Island is a special episode dedicated to Angel Island which is located in the San Francisco Bay.

    It’s the second-largest island in the bay with a ton of history. “Angel Island Immigration Station served as the main immigration facility on the West Coast of the United States from 1910 to 1940,” writes History.
