    GAME RULES

    Mega Millions started out back in 1996.

    Numbers are drawn for the game every Tuesday and Friday.

    Players pick a six number combination out of five white balls and one golden ball, hoping it aligns with the lottery drawing.

    The first set of five numbers are between one and 70, while the Mega Millions number gets picked from one to 25.

    Players can earn a quick buck by simply matching the mega ball with their mega ball number.

    But to win a serious jackpot, players are hoping for a match made in heaven.

    Matching all six numbers results in two options: winners can either get annuity payments or request a lump-sum of the cash prize.

    In Iowa, there's 60 days offered from the date the prize is claimed to decide between the cash and annuity option.
