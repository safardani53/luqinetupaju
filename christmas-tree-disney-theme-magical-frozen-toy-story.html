    MUMS are putting up their Christmas decorations early this year, with a magical twist as people create show-stopping trees inspired by classic Disney films. 

    It seems parents have started decking their homes out for the holidays as they attempt to inject some cheer into a tough year, and they’re merging their two favourite things - Disney and Christmas. 

    Fans of Mickey Mouse, Frozen, Toy Story and other timeless classics have been sharing their incredible Christmas trees online, with show-stopping firs racking up thousands of likes. 

    One mum decided it was finally time to get her decorations out, as she proudly shared her family’s Toy Story-themed tree online.

    Sharing snaps to Facebook group Family Lockdown Tips and Ideas, she said: “November 1, so let’s put up the Christmas Tree. Our Toy Story Christmas tree.

    “This isn’t our main Christmas tree, it’s the kids tree.”

    She explained her son has been collecting toys and decorations inspired his favourite film for some time, saying: “The baubles are from the Disney store over recent years.

    “My son has collected all the toys over the years.” 

    Thousands of people liked the post, and the mum was overwhelmed by the positive feedback.

    She added: “Thank you to everyone who has commented, there are far too many messages to reply to. 

    “It’s really lovely to read all your messages, they’re making us smile.” 

    Commenting on the festive masterpiece, one person said: “Oh my gosh - this is fabulous!"

    Another wrote: “That is incredible! I wouldn't let me kids even breathe near it."

    A third thought: "Absolutely amazing."

    While this person added: "This is unbearably good I'm just not going to bother now this year."

    Fellow movie buffs also shared their handiwork on Facebook, with a tree themed around Spiderman, The Avengers, The Grinch, Minions, Disney princesses and Minnie Mouse.
