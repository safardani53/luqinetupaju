    Apple offers settlement payments - see eligibility

    Large companies commonly are sued for one thing or another and end up settling for a predetermined amount that eligible consumers can get a piece of.

    Those who bought a Macbook between 2015 and 2019 could be entitled to a payout of between $50 and $395.

    Apple was allegedly selling laptops with faulty keyboards during this period but still charging customers to get them fixed at the Genius Bar. The company denies any wrongdoing.

    Mac users in California, Florida, Illinois, Michigan, New Jersey, New York, or Washington may be eligible. They had until March 6 to file a claim on this website.

    Accurately filing taxes, explained

    When an individual is a single parent, for tax purposes they are considered the head of the household.

    As a single parent, you’ll be able to claim a $19,400 standard deduction versus a $12,950 standard deduction for single filers without dependents. There are also separate, more favorable tax brackets for heads of households.

    To be acknowledged as the head of household, you must manually check a box yourself or inform your tax preparer. 

    If you’re married and cover more than half of your child’s expenses, you would also be considered a head of the household but only if you file separately from your spouse.

    Californians could be taxed on 2022 rebate

    If you received a rebate in 2022, you might have to pay taxes on it – and Americans are fuming over it. 

    This might apply if you live in the state of California. 

    The state has been sending rebates under its Middle-Class Tax Refund (MCTR) program since the fall. 

    While the money is providing some relief for high inflation, California’s Franchise Tax Board did issue a warning that is required to issue a 1099-MISC (Miscellaneous Income) tax form if the rebate amount is $600 or more. 

    Find unclaimed money

    Sometimes money gets lost in the transfer after a person dies, sells, or forfeits property.

    South Carolina, Georgia, and other states have their own list of unclaimed properties for residents to cash in on.

    South Carolina State Treasurer Curtis Loftis said: “All sorts of money that people never knew about pops up.”

    Loftis said one woman in South Carolina was owed $1million – that she had no idea about.

    To find out if you have money owed to you, you can check online by searching your name to see if there is a property that matches an area where you have lived or worked.

    By visiting missingmoney.com anyone who is owed cash can claim it easily.

    Private tax filing companies have paid around $39.3million since 2006 to block this initiative.
