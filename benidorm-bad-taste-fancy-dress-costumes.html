    Brit expats and tourists parade in outrageously offensive costumes at Benidorm's annual bad taste

    BRITS have flooded Benidorm dressed in vile outfits for the town's annual &quot;bad taste&quot; fancy dress contest. Tens of thousands of holidaymakers descended on the tourist hotspot to take part in the Costa Blanca's fancy dress day.

    03 Feb 2024, 00:00

    BRITS have flooded Benidorm dressed in vile outfits for the town's annual "bad taste" fancy dress contest.

    Tens of thousands of holidaymakers descended on the tourist hotspot to take part in the Costa Blanca's fancy dress day.

    Among the Brits were two lads in "blackface" costumes and another man who had done the same for a Mel B costume.

    Women wore Loveheart costumes containing racy messages while one dressed as a Monk wearing a sex toy.

    A group of blokes also showed their cheeky side in lacy underwear, while a Borat impersonator was snapped downing beer in the sunshine.

    The event takes place every year the day after the official Benidorm fiesta ends.
