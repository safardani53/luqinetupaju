    Kaitlynn Carter and Kristopher Brock have two children! Carter announced the joyful news on Instagram on February 24 by posting pictures of them in the hospital with the description “… and then there were 5…,” which showed the visage of their newborn daughter. Kaitlynn and Kristopher began courting in May 2020 after being met via messaging by a common acquaintance. After concealing their relationship for more than a year, Kaitlynn gave birth to Rowan, her first child with Kristopher, in September 2021. Brock and his ex-wife, Laura Vassar, have a 7-year-old boy. They separated in 2019. Rowan’s original response to meeting his sister can be seen in another video, where he can be seen smiling and chuckling while cradling the infant.

    Kaitlynn Carter and Kristopher Brock’s Relationship

    In May 2020, a common acquaintance introduced Kaitlynn Carter to Kristopher Brock via messaging. Carter approached Brock for a date on May 18, which happened to be Kristopher’s birthday, and he already had arrangements with his son on that day. On May 19, the pair went on their first date at the Surfrider Hotel Malibu balcony. They did not reveal many specifics about their private relationship at the moment, but Kaitlynn did say:

    “It’s the most natural, relaxed friendship. I’ve never been in a partnership that was simply simple. He feels like the first grownup I’ve ever dated.”

    Kaitlynn Carter of The Hills alluded to her first pregnancy in June 2021 by posting a blurry picture of herself with a belly, but she did not make any public announcements. Rowan, her first child with Kristopher Brock, was born on September 30th, and she announced in a post:

    “Rowan Carter Brock, our big little boy, was born 9/30/21 at 2:53 am, weighing 8 pounds 4 ounces. Everyone is content, robust, and in love.”

    Days before Rowan’s delivery, Kaitlynn’s sibling inadvertently revealed the gender of their first child on Instagram. Kaitlynn discussed having children with her partner on the “Hillscast” show, revealing:

    “For me, when I started courting again, I understood … it was really essential to me to find somebody that had the same objectives and desired the same things in life that I did. We immediately went to the topic of having children and where we stood on everything.”

    They were “on the same page” about the infant, she said. In October 2021, the pair revealed their second pregnancy on Instagram with the message “baby daughter.” Kaitlynn and Kristopher have not named their baby girl.

    Kaitlynn Carter and Kristopher Brock’s previous connection

    In 2019, Kaitlynn Carter ended her relationship with The Hills: New Beginnings actor Brody Jenner. She then dated Miley Cyrus from August to September 2019. In an article, she stated that her connection with Miley was more than just a summer affair; it expanded her senses and assisted her in becoming her “most genuine” self. Carter’s first engagement was with a lady.

    Kristopher Brock and Laura Vassar wedded in 2014 at a Miami service station. Charlie, their boy, was born the same year. Laura applied for divorce in April 2019 without disclosing the cause for their split.
    Kaitlynn Carter and Kristopher Brock’s whereabouts are unclear.
