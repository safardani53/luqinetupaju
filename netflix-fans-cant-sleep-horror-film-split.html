    NETFLIX fans have all complained that they're unable to sleep after watching a creepy horror film called Split.

    The movie, which is available to watch on the popular streaming site, hit our screens in 2016.

    Split follows the story of a man called Kevin Wendell Crumb (James McAvoy) who has dissociative identity disorder (DID).

    Kevin regularly sees his therapist Dr. Karen Fletcher and she had been helping him manage living with 23 personalities.

    In the film viewers see Kevin take on a few of his personalities including Dennis, Patricia, Hedwig, Barry, Orwell, Jade and The Beast.

    During the film Dennis kidnaps three girls and holds them captive in an underground room at the zoo... but the three young girls be able to make it out alive?

    After watching the film, many have taken to Twitter to share their thoughts, with some saying that they found it hard to sleep after watching.

    One said on Twitter: "saw movie split. Can't sleep....great thriller horror....loved seeing my city too."

    Meanwhile others praised actor James AcAvoy for his portrayal in the film.

    Another added: "Split is one of my favorite horror/suspense movies of all time. James McAvoy is unbelievable. This shot is and always will be HAUNTING to me."

    Most read in Film

    "As a horror community, we need to discuss James McAvoy’s performance more in Split. Phenomenal," a third chimed in.

    A fourth added: "It's an edge of your seat kind of movie that dives deep into the mid of someone with DID."

    This isn't the only film that has left Netflix viewers quaking in their boots.

    Others branded the new horror Soft And Quiet ‘one of the most terrifying movies of all time’.

    The film, written and produced by Beth de Araújo, premiered on March 12, 2022, and was later released on November 4 2022.

    It follows the story of a group of women - Emily, Leslie, Kim and Marjorie - who organise a meeting for a new white supremacist group.

    As the film unfolds, they bump into Asian-American sisters Lily and Anne - and things take a very dark turn.

    Meanwhile others were left unable to sleep after watching "grotesque" horror movie Viking Wolf.

    The movie, which is about a teenage girl transforming into a werewolf, includes disturbing scenes of people being torn apart, that have left some fans "terrified".
