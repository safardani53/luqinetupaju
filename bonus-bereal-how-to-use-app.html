    Bonus BeReal: What new features are included in the huge app update?

    PHOTO sharing app BeReal has received the first big update since it burst onto the scene in 2020 - and fans will love it. The platform is finally increasing the number of posts you can put up throughout the day.

    03 May 2024, 00:00

    PHOTO sharing app BeReal has received the first big update since it burst onto the scene in 2020 - and fans will love it.

    The platform is finally increasing the number of posts you can put up throughout the day.

    Users can now opt to share three photos instead of one as part of Bonus BeReal.

    But it's only available to people in the UK for now.

    The move comes amid reports BeReal is losing popularity.

    It went viral for allowing people to show their genuine selves unlike other photo sharing apps, by prompting users to share a photo at a random point in the day.

    Read more about BeReal

    With Bonus BeReal, users involved in the trial will get two more prompts throughout the day.

    They'll still need to post on time - which is just two minutes once the notification has landed.

    How does Bonus BeReal work?

    Bonus BeReal gives you two more opportunities to post in your day.

    However, you'll have to post on time for the first - if not, the other two will be locked for the rest of the day.

    Most read in Tech

    How to find Bonus BeReal

    Bonus BeReal is only available to users in the UK for now as part of a trial.

    If you're eligible, you should see the option to post Bonus BeReal in the frame on the right of your BeReal.

    If you've left it late, you'll get another chance tomorrow.

    Tap the frame to open your camera and take a Bonus BeReal.

    How to delete Bonus BeReal

    Be careful, as deleting your main BeReal of the day will cause your subsequent Bonus BeReal posts to be purged too.

    However, you can delete and repost your Bonus BeReal as much as you like.

  • Tap your Bonus BeReal to open it.
  • Tap the three-dot menu in the top right corner.
  • Tap the delete option. 
  • Select a reason for deleting your Bonus BeReal and confirm.
