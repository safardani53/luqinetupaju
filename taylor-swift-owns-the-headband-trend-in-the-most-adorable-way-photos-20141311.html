    Headbands are here to play!

    Although you might have been in elementary school the last time you wore a headband (and it probably had a flower stuck to it), over the past few weeks, celebrities have been bringing the school-days trend back in a very adult way.

    For a shopping date with bestie Karlie Kloss on Wednesday, Nov. 12, in NYC, Taylor Swift topped off her tartan ensemble with a gold headband. And, because the “Blank Space” singer, 24, is known to love all things adorable, hers was accented with a petite golden bow.

    But just two days before, Jennifer Lawrence worked one, too, at the premiere of The Hunger Games: Mockingjay – Part 1 in London. The Oscar winner, 24, turned it out on the red carpet in a breezy quilted dress by Christian Dior. But when it came to her beauty look, Lawrence made sure that her grown-out pixie stayed put with a skinny black band.

    And finally, on Oct. 24, AnnaSophia Robb attended the 2014 Lang Lang & Friends Gala at the United Nations Headquarters in an LBD and one adorable black pearl hairpiece. The former Carrie Diaries star even added matching pearl earrings to her look.

    Each lady worked her headband in a unique way. We’d love to try them all, and we thought you might, too. So here are some close matches: For a bow-accented gold headband like Swift’s, shop the Uncommon Character Headband ($11, Modcloth.com).
