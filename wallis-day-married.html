    Most recently, she joined Season 2 of Sex/Life, and fans are curious: Is Wallis married?

    Is Wallis Day married?

    Wallis's posts on her Instagram feature many people in them, so it's not often easy to tell if she's dating anyone in particular.

    There's a mystery man she calls "mon cherí" in one Instagram photo, in which she can also be spotted wearing (what seems like) a wedding ring. The person in the photo isn't tagged, but rumor has it that his name is George King, whom she has been dating since 2019.
