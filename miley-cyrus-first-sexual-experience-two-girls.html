    Eighties treat so sweet

    MILEY returned today with her first single in a year — called Midnight Sky and inspired by the Eighties.

    The superstar, who wore several striking outfits and rolled in a pool of sweets in the eye-catching video, explained: “I wanted a song which felt classic.

    “I wanted a song that felt like it was from the era which I usually pull my inspiration from – but really not being afraid, really being unapologetic about the tone, the sonics and the lyrics.”

    Miley hasn’t just made an Eighties-inspired song — she’s also got a mullet to match.

    She said: “When I cut my hair, I told my mum, ‘Make me look like Rod Stewart’.” But the track may not end up on a new album. On where she is at the moment musically, she added: “I really have always enjoyed dropping singles rather than the full body of work, because it allows you to talk to your fans in real time.

    “When you write a record, a lot of the time you’re writing your experiences, and then by the time the record comes out, you’ve evolved past that experience.”
