    90 DAY Fiancé star Larissa Lima announced she has been fired from the hit TLC show after she did a revealing lingerie livestream with CamSoda. 

    On September 14, the 33-year-old did a racy session on the adult webcam platform - which often features live porn - after reportedly getting nearly $75,000 of plastic surgery. 

    To show off the results of her breast implants, butt augmentation and nose job, Larissa appeared in a NSFW video on the website. 

    The one-hour show featured the model in sexy underwear and a g-string bikini. 

    Less than two weeks later, the reality star revealed she’s been axed from the television series.

    “Dear Friends and Followers, I want to make a statement for you, I’m no longer a cast member of the show 90 Day Fiancé,” she wrote on Instagram on Saturday. 

    Larissa first appeared on season six of the reality series with her ex-husband, Colt Johnson. 

    The Brazilian native continued: “This is my final statement. 

    “Because of my show with CamSoda, I was released from my contract with TLC by phone the day before ICE arrested me. 

    “I will continue producing content for my official channels on Instagram, OnlyFans and Youtube. Thanks for your understanding, love and attention.”

    Earlier this month, Larissa was arrested by ICE at her Las Vegas home she shares with boyfriend Eric Nichols, TMZ first reported. 

    At the time of her detainment, the star’s rep said: “Larissa's legal team is working meticulously on her release and clearing up this misunderstanding.”

    She was released soon after and revealed she plans to move to Colorado Springs with her boyfriend. 

    After news of her firing was made public, Larissa reposted a clip her boyfriend posted that read “finally free” with Tom Petty’s Free Falling playing in the background.

    According to TMZ, Larissa underwent $72,000 worth of plastic surgery in February with the goal of appearing to look like Kylie Jenner. 

    The outlet claimed she took her breast size up significantly from a 34A to a 34DD. 

    She also reportedly underwent liposuction on her abs and waist - with the three pounds of fat removed from her stomach injected into her butt. 

    Though her career on 90 Day Fiancé are numbered, Larissa is optimistic with continuing her work on her OnlyFans page.

    As for what’s next, she teased fans in May about the possibility of pursuing a career in porn but shut down rumors she was headed in that direction. 

    She told her followers during an Instagram Livestream: "People are posting in the press that I’m going to be a porn star.

    "No, I’m not going to be a porn star. Half a million dollars. $500,000, I would do.”

    However, she assured her fans that becoming a porn star was not on the agenda for her.

    She explained: "I came [on Instagram Live] because I got a lot of questions, this and that, people posting that I was going to be a porn star.

    "Driving me nuts. It’s not true.”
