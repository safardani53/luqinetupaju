    21 May 2024, 00:00

    Not backing down one bit! Khloé Kardashian took to Twitter on Monday, July 18, to defend herself against those who criticized her for attacking Chloe Grace Moretz in defense of sister Kim Kardashian. (Watch the video above to see the controversial Snapchats that launched the feud!)

    “I’m the last person to bully but have an animal instinct to protect and defend my family,” the Strong Looks Better Naked author, 32, wrote. “Refrain from commenting on my family and OUR business (and I will do the same).”

    As previously reported, Khloé clapped back at the 19-year-old actress after she spoke out in favor of Swift, 26, in the “Shake It Off” singer’s ongoing feud with Kim. Bad blood began to boil again on Sunday, July 17, after the Selfish author took to Snapchat to share a recorded conversation between husband Kanye West and Swift.

    In the various clips, the pop star seemingly gives Ye approval to use her name in his controversial song “Famous,” in which he raps: “I feel like me and Taylor still might have sex / Why? I made that bitch famous.” Swift’s team had previously denied that such a call took place.

    In response to Kim’s siege against T. Swift, Moretz tweeted: “Stop wasting your voice on something so petulant and unimportant. Everyone in this industry needs to get their heads out of a hole and look around to realize what’s ACTUALLY happening in the REAL world.”

    In defense of her older sibling, Khloé hastily replied to Moretz by sending her a very explicit tweet featuring a side-by-side collage of the Neighbors 2: Sorority Rising star in a red bikini next to a pic of a woman who resembled the actress wearing a similar swimsuit. “Is this the a hole you’re referring to @ChloeGMoretz ???” the Keeping Up With the Kardashians personality taunted.

    Moretz — who previously had beef with the Kardashian clan back in March when she condemned Kim for her infamous nude selfie — was quick to call Khloé out: “@khloekardashian fact check: first photo is me filming my movie Neighbors 2, the second photo is some girl who was wrongfully photographed.”

    Aside from the If I Stay actress putting KoKo in her place, Twitter lashed out at the reality star for her social media snafu.

    “oh sweetie, thats not her. stop humiliating yourself LMAOOOOO,” one online user wrote, while another simply stated: “TOO FAR.”

    “ahhh! Khloe, I thought you was the nice one of the Kardashians. Out of order that…. You’re a grown woman,” one tweeter lamented, while another sniped: “old ass divorcee beefing with a teenager.”
