    05 Jun 2024, 00:00

    LEICESTER CITY fans are raging over their new white away kit - because of its similarity to a £15 shirt from Adidas.

    The kit manufacturer latest jersey release, priced at £55, appears similar to an alternative top they sell  called the Regista but with the Foxes' badge and sponsor included.

    Leicester have already unveiled their traditional blue home kit, while also going with a grey strip when playing away.

    But their latest jersey has come under fire for its simplicity and likeness to a cheaper training top from the German manufacturers.

    Some have hinted that it was rushed to be ready in time for the new season, with the Premier League campaign kicking off in eight days' time.

    While the difference in price was an obvious criticism with Adidas charging four times more for Leicester's new strip, compared to their Regista top.
