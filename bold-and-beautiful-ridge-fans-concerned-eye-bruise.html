    THE Bold and the Beautiful fans have become concerned after spotting a strange detail on Ridge Forrester’s face in recent episodes.

    Thorsten Kaye has been portraying the character on the long-running soap opera since 2013.

    Over the past week, some eagle-eyed viewers have noticed something strange about Ridge’s face.

    They’ve pointed out that there appears to be a cut or a bruise under one of the character’s eyes.

    Fans started noticing the possible injury while watching new episodes of The Bold and the Beautiful that aired late last week.

    After watching Thursday’s broadcast, one concerned viewer asked online: “Was Ridge socked in the eye? Looks like there's a bruise there.”
