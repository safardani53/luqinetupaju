    BEAUTY fans are going wild over new sale at Boots where you can nab hand creams for less than 50p - but there's a slight catch.

    What's better than a successful beauty haul at Boots? One where you save heaps of money - and now one shopper, Karen Deborah Davies, has shared how she bagged goodies on the cheap.

    The delighted customer, who shared the news on Facebook, got her hands on two of beauty buys by the brand No.7.

    These, she revealed in the post, were a black mascara, as well as a small tub of a lip and cheek tint in a summery peach shade.

    The total, according to Boots' website, should've come to almost £24 - but at the till, Karen paid more almost 50 per cent less.

    ''All this for £13.90,'' she claimed on the popular Extreme Couponing and Bargains UK group.

    read more on beauty

    According to Karen, the high street retailer was running an offer where you can claim £10 off if you spend £20.

    To make the mini shopping spree even better, the lucky Boots' customer even got a freebie - a lip and eye palette that was worth £25.

    The Latest Deals reports that the deal is only valid for No.7 products - and Boots website states that you can now also get 20 per cent off on premium beauty buys too.

    These include some of our cult favourite products, such as Bobbi Brown Vitamin Enriched Face Base, Laura Mercier Translucent Loose Setting Powder, ELEMIS Pro-Collagen Cleansing Balm, as well as Sol de Janeiro Brazilian Crush Cheirosa 62 Perfume Mist.
