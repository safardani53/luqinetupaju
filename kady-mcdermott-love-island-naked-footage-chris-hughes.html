    KADY McDermott has expressed her disgust that footage of Love Island's Chris Hughes naked in the shower was mysteriously leaked online.

    The star of the 2016 series said it was "so invasive" and that she'd be "horrified" if it happened to her.

    Speaking to us at the The Sun's Love Island Finale Party, Kady revealed she used to shower "with a towel over the door" because she was scared people would see her naked.

    "It's so bad, it's awful," she exclusively told The Sun Online.

    "That's horrible. Imagine if that was a girl... I think people think it's OK because it's boys but it's not. If it was me naked I'd sue someone.

    "I think it's awful. It's not on."

    The intimate footage of Chris was leaked online earlier this month and showed the Love Island star walking around the villa with his penis exposed.

    TV bosses stepped in to have the previously un-aired footage removed from social media.

    MORE FROM THE VILLA

    The footage, which looks to have been filmed by a mobile phone from a screen, shows Chris walking in the bathroom with his shorts pulled down and his penis exposed, talking to someone else.

    "What, it's a d***?" he says before he is told to get into the shower.

    A show source told The Sun Online: "We are aware of certain footage being in circulation on the internet and we have taken steps to remove it."

    However, a source close to Chris revealed they were "hugely disappointed" that the footage had been leaked online.

    Chris wasn't the only victim as an uncensored picture of Jamie Jewitt performing a strip tease on the show also found its way online.

    In the snap, which appeared on Twitter, Jamie is seen dancing around nude with his penis in full view of the camera.

    Rachel looked gorgeous in a plunging pale pink body-con dress and wore her long blonde hair in curls.

    It was an eventful episode that saw Camilla say "I love you" to Jamie for the first time.

    They've lasted two months without it and Gabby and Marcel said they can't wait to have sex after leaving the villa.

    However, fans were convinced that Kem and Chris were the REAL winners and shared hilarious memes of their bromance.

    The boys are even in talks to for their own record deal after impressing fans with their raps in the villa.

    Of course viewers were upset that the show has come to an end, which resulted in a flurry of funny GIFs about how they're struggling to cope without their favourite reality show.
