    Michael Bublé and his wife, Luisana Lopilato, tied the knot in March 2011 and have been sharing their major family milestones ever since.

    The couple welcomed their first son, Noah, the year after their nuptials. His siblings, Elias and Vida, arrived in 2014 and 2018, respectively.

    In 2016, Noah was diagnosed with liver cancer. Although the little one recovered after chemotherapy and radiation treatments, Bublé emotionally told James Corden during a Carpool Karaoke appearance he still wasn’t “OK.”

    He explained, “My wife honestly, when this all started, I became the strength to somehow pull us and lift us and to be positive … and when they got it out, and the chemo was done and they said, ‘We did it, it’s good, he’s OK,’ I just fell. I fell and my wife picks me up now.”
