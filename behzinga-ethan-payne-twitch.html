    Who is Ethan 'Behzinga' Payne?

    Behzinga is a British internet personality who started his streaming journey ten years ago while in school.

    His real name is Ethan Payne and he was born in London.

    His earlier content focused on Call of Duty and Fifa, but since then he's evolved into many more areas.
