    Grab that pumpkin spice latte and break out the jack-o’-lanterns. Freeform is fully prepared to get us in the Halloween spirit early. On August 31, the network announced its 2023 31 Nights of Halloween schedule. The programming event is celebrating its milestone 25th anniversary.
