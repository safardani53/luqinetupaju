    However, the NFL player deleted his social media handles in late 2019, presumably to keep his personal life away from the spotlight.

    "Can't blame him, people treat athletes like absolute garbage," a Reddit user wrote, implying the football wide receiver also decided to avoid unnecessary criticisms about his performances from netizens.

    The user continued to write, "He obviously hasn't been good, but he's still a human."

    Meanwhile, due to his private lifestyle at present, fans are usually seen inquiring if he got married to a wife or not.

    Nelson Agholor Isn't Reported to Have a Wife

    Despite inquiries from fans regarding his marriage status, there has been no official word from Agholor or any trusted source so far.

    Hence, it would be unfair to speculate that the NFL star is married to a wife now and keeping it a secret — or any such assumption would just be baseless.

    Meanwhile, there have also been questions on whether Agholor is still dating his once girlfriend Volpicelli, who many fans see as the player's future wife.
