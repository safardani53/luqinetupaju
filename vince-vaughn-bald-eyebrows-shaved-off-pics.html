    Vince Vaughn, 46, has drastically changed his look up. The actor debuted a wild new hairstyle on Thursday, September 8, in the sense that he doesn’t have a single strand of it left on his head. No, really… even his eyebrows appear to have been shaved off!

    Vince’s bald head was a real shock to the world, especially since just days before he was sporting a full head of hair on the red carpet at the Venice Film Festival. However, now that he’s back stateside, Vince is preparing to film his next movie, Brawl In Cell Block 99, which is why he shaved his head, reports ET Online.

    [jwplatform mgUzG7a7]

    As for the new movie, it’s an action flick that tells the story of a former boxer whose life falls apart at the seams, reports our sister site Variety. After losing his job and struggling to keep his marriage together he accepts a job as a drug courier, though it eventually lands him behind bars with some not-so-nice people. It sounds like it’s going to be an intense one, and we can’t wait to see bald Vince in action!
