    How old is Willow Smith?

    Willow Camille Reign Smith was born in Los Angeles, California on October 31, 2000.

    The 21-year-old is a singer, songwriter, rapper, record producer, actress, and dancer.

    Willow is the daughter of Will Smith and Jada Pinkett Smith, and the younger sister of rapper, singer, and actor Jaden Smith.

    Willow made her acting debut in 2007 in the film I Am Legend and later appeared in Kit Kittredge: An American Girl.
