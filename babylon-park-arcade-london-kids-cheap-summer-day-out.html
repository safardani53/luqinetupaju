    A WOMAN has revealed that she's found the coolest place to take your kids to this summer.

    So if you’re struggling to think of fun ways to keep your little darlings entertained during the six-week holidays, you’ll need to listen up.

    Serena recently took a day trip to Babylon Park in Camden, London - an amusement park with roller coasters, rides and arcade games.

    There’s dodgems and axe throwing too and it is free to enter the indoor arcade.

    Posting on social media, Serena shared a clip from her fun day out.

    She described Babylon Park as “the coolest arcade in London”, as she explained: “Went here with my best friends and had the best time.”

    “If you’re looking for somewhere to bring your kids this summer, then bring them here.

    “You can top up your (tokens) cards with as little as £1 at this arcade.

    “I’ve never seen so many rides and roller coasters in an arcade.
