    The top cocktail recipe books in 2023 are an excellent addition to any recipe book collection. If you love hosting parties or want to make your date night more interesting, you should purchase a cocktail recipe book.

    A cocktail recipe book is an affordable item that can make the world of difference to your hosting events. Cocktail recipe books will teach you how to make some classics as well as some new creations. They will also give you advice on what accessories to purchase and how to best present a cocktail.

    There are many cocktail recipe books on the market and choosing the best one might seem like a difficult task. However, there are several considerations you can make to narrow down your search. Continue reading to see what we suggest.

    Things to Consider Before Purchasing a Cocktail Recipe Book

    Number of cocktails

    Consider how many cocktails the recipe book contains. The more recipes the better. However, more recipes mean a larger book, or smaller print and pictures.

    Size

    Choosing a small cocktail recipe book will allow you to take the book to the store. This saves you from making a shopping list of the ingredients you will need. Alternatively, you may prefer a larger book that will look stylish in your home.

    Cocktails you love

    Choosing a cocktail recipe book containing some of the cocktails you love means it is likely the book will contain other cocktails that will match your tastes.

    Cocktails youâ€™ve never heard of

    On the other hand, you could choose a recipe book containing more recipes you do not know. You may want to purchase a book with cocktails you have heard of. Alternatively, you can choose a book full of cocktails you have never heard of but look interesting to you.

    Easy to follow table of contents

    There should be a clear table of contents in the book, so you can easily find a specific recipe. Cocktails may be listed alphabetically. They may also be listed by the spirit they contain, or the flavor, such as sweet, sour, or creamy.

    Large, easily visible writing

    Choose a book with easy-to-see writing. Large print options may be available if needed. This is especially important if you will be enjoying a cocktail or two while creating more cocktails.

    Beautiful photographs and artwork

    The cocktail recipe book should be pleasant to look at. The book should visually display the finished cocktail, so you can see how best to present it. Some books have step-by-step instructions in photograph or illustration form.

    Particular spirit-based recipes

    If there is a particular spirit you prefer over other spirits, you could look for a cocktail recipe book with recipes that solely use that spirit. Gin cocktail recipe books have become particularly popular in recent years.

    Cocktails to suit all tastes

    Consider how much you will use the book to make cocktails for other people. If you know what sort of cocktails your loved ones enjoy, consider looking for a book containing the recipes for those cocktails. It is likely you will entertain people in the future who will appreciate a wide range of choices.

    Non-alcoholic cocktails

    It is always useful to have some non-alcoholic cocktail options for the days you do not feel like drinking alcohol. You can make these with the kids or grandkids as a fun activity.

    Bartender hacks

    Some recipe books include bartender hacks that allow you to cut corners when you are short of ingredients or time.

    Easy to source ingredients

    Consider the ingredients used in the recipe book. Think about how easily accessible the ingredients are. If you choose a recipe book full of expensive and difficult-to-source ingredients, you will not use the book very often. Opt for a book with mostly affordable ingredients with a few more expensive and exotic cocktail recipes included.

    Writer of the recipe book

    Many people choose a recipe book based on who the author is. If there is a particular celebrity chef you love, you could search the internet to see if they have made a cocktail recipe book.

    Space for your own recipes

    You may want to look for a recipe book with blank pages at the end so you can write down some of your own cocktail recipes.

    Binder

    Choosing a binder-type cocktail recipe book will allow you to add pages to the book. This means you can print recipes from the internet, or write down your own, and place them in a location that is well ordered.

    Cocktail Recipe Book: Price Range

    A cocktail recipe book can cost from as little as $7 and as much as $30. You can also get cocktail recipe books included in Kindle and other Ebook subscription services.

    How We Choose the Top Cocktail Recipe Books

    To choose the top cocktail recipe books we consider the following:

    • Number of cocktails
    • How nice the cocktails taste
    • Presentation
    • Easy-to-follow instructions
    • Cost
    • Customer reviews
