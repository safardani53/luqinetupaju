    06 Jun 2024, 00:00

    LOVE Island faced a new scandal last night after a video emerged of contestant Will Young partying with what appears to be a bag of white powder.

    The TikTok star, 23 — known as Farmer Will — was filmed seemingly pulling a small pouch from his man-bag while in a swimming pool at a festival with pals.

    Surrounded by dozens of revellers in the footage, he looks around before continuing to dance while holding the mystery substance.

    Topless Will, in black shorts with sunglasses on his head, then turns away from the camera and looks down with his hands together.

    He then gets the attention of a pal in a pink bucket hat.

    Will seemingly puts something towards his pal’s mouth, then raves a bit more before the 48-second clip ends.

    The video is another headache for ITV bosses after The Sun this week published a video of evicted thug Islander Haris Namani, 21, punching a man in a street brawl.

    One reveller said: “I recognised Will straight away as I knew him from TikTok.

    “He was cutting some shapes and looked like he was having a great time.

    “Then he pulled out the bag and carried on dancing while he was holding it.

    "He tried to give something to his mate but I’m not sure what it was or whether he took it.

    “I bumped into him and he’s a decent bloke. He’s very much a party boy.”

    The video shows Will at Noa Beach Club, one of five open-air venues which host 20,000 at the five-day Hideout Festival in Croatia.

    Witnesses at last July’s event in Zrce did not confirm any law-breaking.

    Will, from Aylesbury, Bucks, has 1.4million followers on social media site TikTok, where he became popular by sharing clips of daily life on the farm where he grew up.

    He and Aussie bombshell Jessie Wynter, 26, are second favourites to win the show.

    ITV declined to comment on the video.
