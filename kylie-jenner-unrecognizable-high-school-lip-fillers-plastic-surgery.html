    KYLIE Jenner fans were shocked at her "natural" appearance in a series of throwback photos recently rediscovered online.

    In a plethora of pics snapped between 2011 and 2013, the soon-to-be reality star shined without her lip fillers or plastic surgery makeovers.

    In an online chat room, fans of Kylie, now 24, shared and discussed her old school look, with photos taken while she was actually still in school.

    The photos, which Kylie shared herself over the year, included a look at her yearbook photo, several selfies and snaps with friends like jordyn woods and sofia richie.

    The filter-free photos saddened fans, who posted in the chat room regrets over the cosmetic work the reality star has had.

    "She looked so much better before. Wow," one fan exclaimed. "She didn’t need much of anything done."

    Another pointed out: "I swear she became simultaneously happy AND unhappy when she got the fillers. Her eyes are definitely not so bright anymore."

    Kylie’s pout looked as big as ever as she rubbed in the gloss with her fingers.

    Holding the new products, she posed with her lips puckered to show off the results.

    KYLIE'S CONFESSION

    Kylie admitted in 2015 to getting lip fillers when she was 17 years old after dealing with insecurities about her "small lips" for years.

    She previously said: "I was 15 and I was insecure about my lips.

    "I had really small lips, and it was, like, one of my first kisses, and a guy was like, 'I didn't think you'd be a good kisser because you have such small lips.'

    "I took that really hard. Just when a guy you like says that... I don't know, it just really affected me. I didn't feel desirable or pretty.

    "I would overline my lips with the lip liner to create the illusion of bigger lips and then, finally, I was like, this lip liner isn't doing it and ended up getting my lips done. It sticks with you - it just got in there."

    RUMOR HAS IT

    Rumours that Kylie - who has a bestselling lip liner range - had plumped her pout began back in 2014 but she initially brushed over doing anything to enhance her appearance.

    In an interview with E! News that year, she said: "I feel like everyone has been talking about it for months, so I'm kind of sick of it.
