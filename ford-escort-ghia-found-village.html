    A CAR enthusiast has highlighted an old Ford Escort he found in an immaculate condition hidden away in his village.

    The TikTok user known as “@cxyzshoots” took to the social media account to show off the silver Ford Escort Ghia in all its glory.

    It’s not clear which village the car was found in but the TikToker’s profile says he comes from Somerset.

    The short clip is wordless but shows off the car’s sleek design from all angles as it’s parked up on the pavement on what appears to be a residential street.

    Comments although brief clearly showed their admiration.
