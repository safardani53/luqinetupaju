    WE ALL know the worst part about cooking any meal is having to clean up afterwards.

    But one savvy mum shared how you can quickly whip up a tasty omelette in the Air Fryer and make no mess at all.

    Kim Paucknet shared the genius trick on her TikTok account (@bigmommakimbo7) and people can't believe how easy it is.

    So if you want to give the delicious dish a god for yourself start by grabbing some liners to go in your Air Fryer.

    Kim used parchment paper liners, which you can buy on Amazon, but there are also silicone liners you can use.

    One the liner is in, add a splash of oil and crack five eggs directly into the tray.

    Mix them together with a fork and add any vegetables of your choosing.

    The foodie went with spinach, tomatoes and grated cheese, not forgetting to add a dash of pepper.

    She then left it to cook in the Air Fryer at 375°F (190°C) for 20 minutes.

    After 20 minutes the omelette was cooked to perfection.

    And the best part is there was no mess left in the Air Fryer tray, thanks to the parchment liner.

    The the frittata style omelette looked so delicious and fellow foodies had no idea it was so easy to cook them in the Air Fryer.

    One commented: "Where have you been my whole Air Fryer life?"

    A second said: "How did I not know about Air Fryer liners?!"

    "Wow I have to do this, I hate cooking eggs," another wrote.
