    TODAY host Craig Melvin has revealed his latest charitable career move in a new social media post.

    The Today star shared his big news in a very personal Instagram post on Friday.

    In the post, Craig, 44, shared a photo of himself and his NFL reporter wife Lindsay Czarniak.

    Alongside the image, he revealed: "Lindsay and I are thrilled to once again host The Bottoms Up Invitational concert and golf tournament September 24-25 in Connecticut to benefit the Colorectal Cancer Alliance."

    He continued: "This event is in honor of my brother Lawrence, who died from colorectal cancer three years ago, and all those affected by this disease.

    "I hope you’ll join us for an incredible concert one month from today with Hootie & the Blowfish, Branford Marsalis, Javier Colon, Chris Barron, Ray Bourdreaux, Edwin McCain and Preston Pohl."
