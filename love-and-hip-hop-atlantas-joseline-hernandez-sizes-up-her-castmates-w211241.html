    Love and Hip Hop: Atlantas Joseline Hernandez Sizes Up Her Castmates

    11 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Get with the winning team, yo! Joseline Hernandez, AKA the Puerto Rican Princess, swung by Us Weekly’s New York City offices to deem her Love & Hip Hop: Atlanta castmates Royalty or Rejects. So who’s worthy to remain in her court, and who’s banished from the kingdom? Watch the video above to find out!

    Hernandez, 29, has been a mainstay on VH1’s Love & Hip Hop: Atlanta since its debut season in 2012. So the “Girls Gone Wild” rapper is pretty familiar with her fellow OGs, including Rasheeda Frost — a reject, in her book. “She’s just there,” Hernandez said dismissively. “You gotta have some spice … you gotta be able to stand out.”

    The partner of music producer and castmate Stevie J. told Us that she’s friendly with most of the guys on the show, including rapper Scrappy. “He’s cool!” she said.

    Another member of the winning royalty team? Musician and friend K. Michelle, who’s back on the show after leaving season two in 2013 for her own spinoff, K. Michelle: My Life. “She does great work,” Hernandez praised. “She’s a workaholic like myself.”

    But Hernandez still isn’t too thrilled about Jessica Dime, with whom who she has a longstanding beef. Hernandez and Dime were both strippers at Miami’s King of Diamonds before the show, and had an intimate relationship. “Once my bitch, always my bitch,” Hernandez sniped.

    There was no comment on Stevie J, who revealed that he and Hernandez were never legally married on the Monday, June 20 episode. Fans will have to tune in to VH1’s Love & Hip Hop: Atlanta (on Mondays at 8 p.m. EST) to watch that saga unfold! Watch Hernandez tell all in the video now.
