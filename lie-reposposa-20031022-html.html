    Pinoy Big Brother: Otso is Pinoy Big Brother's eighth major season, premiered on November 10, 2018 and ending August 4, 2019 on ABS-CBN. Alex Gonzaga, the host of All In, has returned to the show with former winners Kim Chiu and Melai Cantiveros as the new host. Bianca Gonzalez reprized her role as host when joining Batch 2 Star Dreamers. Four awards by roommates, two adult civilians and two groups of teenagers, competed for their respective prizes. In the fifth lot, all finalists from each batch return to compete for eight spots in the final. The show ended with Yamyam Gucong proclaiming the winner of the entire season.
