    What does your ideal date look like? A candlelight dinner accompanied by live music? Have you ever imagined what if your date is the one who is performing live? Well, a similar incident happened recently in Chiltern Firehouse. You might be wondering who was it, here’s your answer:

    It was none other than singer Geri Halliwell who gave her first Tv performance and later enjoyed the night out with her husband, formula 1 King Christian Horner. Although it was not any kind of fancy date with candles and dinner, the couple surely enjoyed the night. Read more:

    Geri Halliwell spotted with husband Christian Horner

    44-year-old Geri looked full of enthusiasm when she was seen leaving the celeb haunt Chiltern Firehouse with her husband Christian. She wore a little black dress which made her complexion glow.

    Geri Halliwell spotted with husband Christian Horner

    She looked lovely as she walked holding her husband’s hand.

    Geri Halliwell first TV performance

    Inside the Firehouse, Geri performed George Michael's tribute song ‘’ and it was her first TV performance. It was a tribute to one of her very close friend to whom she often coined as "the best kind of friend anyone could have".

    Geri was rather close to Michael as she revealed how he was always there whenever she needed him. Although Geri didn’t see him often, he was one of those people who stood by her side in difficult times. She explains her relationship with Michael as:

    ‘He stood up for me and he took me in and I lived with him when I had my first child, and also when I left the Spice Girls I lived with him. So moments when I felt vulnerable. And also with music I would go and say, 'What do you think?' He meant a lot to me.’

    Geri Halliwell affected by George Michael's death

    Sadly, Michael is no longer with her as he passed away on 2016’s Christmas. She felt like a walking wounded when she found out about her friend’s death through Twitter.

    Geri Halliwell with husband Christian Horner during her pregnancy

    During that time, Michael was pregnant and the news made her hormones all over the place which made things hard to process.

    Geri Halliwell relationship with Christian Horner

    Geri has been in a relationship with her husband for a long time. She first began dating Christian in February 2014. They announced their engagement on 11th November 2014. Eventually, the pair tied the knot on 15th May 2015. They exchanged vows at St Mary's Church in Woburn, Bedfordshire.

     
    Geri is the mother of two kids. She has a daughter named Bluebell Madonna from her former relationship with boyfriend Sacha Gervasi. However, she gave birth to her first child with Christian, whom they named Montague George Hector Horner on 21st January 2017.

    Geri Halliwell with husband Christian Horner

    Although Geri has been married to husband Christian for 3 years, their married life has only seen good days. There has arrived no any rumors of divorce between them. They chemistry between them make it look like their relationship will last forever.
