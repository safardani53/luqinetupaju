    Snooki got a jump on her competition …

    Snooki Polizzi will help ring in 2011 with a ball drop spectacle on MTV tonight. Initially it was to have taken place live at Times Square. Within the past few days that idea was nixed and the event was filmed last night at the Jersey Shore.

    The video will be shown tonight during MTV’s New Year’s Eve Bash 2011. A show rep said: “We love our Times Square home and while we’re disappointed there won’t be a Snooki ball drop there, she cannot be denied! So we’re taking a road trip to the place where it all began, Seaside Heights, and droppin’ it all there.”
