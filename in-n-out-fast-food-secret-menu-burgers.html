    IN-N-OUT fans have been going wild after realizing they can order two secret menu items from the famed burger chain.

    The fast food giant In-N-Out began as California’s first drive-thru hamburger stand in 1948 and is now a national icon.

    Currently operating nearly 400 units, the fast food joint is known for its signature burgers, fries and shakes.

    However, as one In-N-Out fan revealed in a new TikTok video, there are ways to order delicious meals that aren’t even listed on the menu.

    TikToker Jordan (@Jordan_The_Stallion_8) shared the secret tip to uncover a new delicious version of the classic In-N-Out burger.

    Jordan told followers you can actually get the burger with these unique customizations: tomato, onion, or pickle wrapped.

    He took his followers on the trip as he tested out just how tasty the special burgers would be.

    “I’m going to get a few of them, and we’ll try them,” Jordan said.

    He did have one caveat, however.

    “Look, I’m an adult so I’m allowed to say I don’t like tomatoes,” he shared.

    “I don’t think I ever have. The last time I tasted a tomato was about five years ago so we’ll try it. Everyone says it’s delicious.”

    Once Jordan had the tomato wrapped burger in hand, he was excited to get started and rate the $3 entree.

    “I really don’t like tomatoes, but we’ll rate it,” he said.

    He had a surprising reaction to the secret menu item.

    “I’m not going to lie,” Jordan said. “At first I thought I really wasn’t going to like it, but turns out the after taste is really good.”

    Altogether, the tomato-wrapped burger earned a score of eight out of 10, the TikToker said.

    Next up was the onion-wrapped burger.

    In this sandwich, the burger is placed between two grilled onions as replacements for the traditional burger bun.

    After trying the cheesy burger, the content creator nearly fell over in delight.

    “This one,” he said. “Get this one.”

    Fans of the chain have reacted to the onion-packed sandwich on Reddit with various levels of excitement.

    “Please I need it,” one person commented.

    Another one wrote: “So simple yet I never would've thought to do it.”

    FAST FOOD NEWS

    While many fast food chains offer a discreet “secret menu,” several companies are also launching menu items with brand new ingredients.

    For instance, at Taco Bell, the Volcano Menu is back.

    The offerings initially made their way into stores in the 1990s but were completely gone by 2016.

    But starting Thursday, customers will once again be able to enjoy the Volcano taco and burrito.

    As part of the menu, guests also have the option to add Lava sauce to any item at Taco Bell.

    Meanwhile, another burger chain, Smashburger has launched a brand new decadent meal for the summer season.

    The double Carolina BBQ burnt ends burger features two beef patties topped with bacon strips, the tips of which have been slightly burned.

    Then, in between the meat, customers will taste delicious melted cheddar cheese and a generous portion of pickles.

    Carl’s Jr has also been stirring up interest with the return of the El Diablo burger.

    Customers had been begging and petitioning the chain to put it back on menus since it was discontinued in 2018.

    The El Diablo burger features a charbroiled beef patty topped with a fiery habanero sauce, bacon, jalapeno popper bites, pepper jack cheese, and jalapenos.
