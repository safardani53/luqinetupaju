    A BELOVED Italian eatery announced it would be closing down after less than a year in business.

    Via Sofia's in Rockford, Illinois dazzled foodies with its menu of Italian delights but anyone looking to test their palate has missed out, as the restaurant's last day of operation was April 1.

    "Dear Via Sofia customers and friends. We are saddened to share the news that the Via Sofia's Italian Kitchen Rockford store is closing," read a statement on the restaurant's Facebook page.

    "The decision was made based on labor constraints in the market and other business challenges that restaurants face. We have had a pleasure serving our Belvidere and Rockford neighbors, and we will miss you."

    The owners thanked the employees "who put in a lot of hard work and dedication to feeding the community our made-from-scratch Italian recipes with love.

    "Thank you to everyone who supported our small business through this journey."
