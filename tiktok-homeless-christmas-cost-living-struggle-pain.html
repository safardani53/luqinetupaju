    A HOMELESS woman is one of the many who will find Christmas a difficult time this year as she struggles without family.

    JP, who goes by the online name kevinthepigeonnn0, posted a TikTok revealing her predicament as she says she is "slowly deteriorating".

    The 23-year-old American sits in her car as she records herself to the audio of John Lennon's classic Christmas hit, Happy Xmas (War Is Over).

    "My brain can't even recognize that it's Christmas when I'm living in my car, haven't spoken to or seen my brother in 2 years officially this week and it's my first Christmas without my grandma."

    JP has used the hashtags "christmas", "nocontact", "mentalhealth", and "grief" with the eight-second clip.

    JP's other videos reveal the woman also battles addiction and has had her heart broken by an ex-boyfriend.
