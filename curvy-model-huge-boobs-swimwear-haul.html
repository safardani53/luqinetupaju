    A CURVY creator has shared her swimwear haul, including a flattering one-piece.

    The influencer reviewed the bathing suits to see if they were big-bust approved.

    In her video, TikTok user Raeann Langas (@raeannlangas) explained: "I am reviewing Cuup Swim, which is for the big boob girls."

    For her first look, the TikToker modeled a $158 black balconette and brief swim set.

    "The boob support is there but these bottoms, if I pull them up like this so they’re a more flattering cut and they just fall down. I hate them," she said.

    In the comments section, the influencer added: "I think the high waist would be better!"

    Raeann continued: "Let’s talk about the top. This gives you really good support, the fabric is very thick, and these bands could hold a lot."

    The curvy model also posed in a $188 scoop neck one-piece in the color Tide.

    She said: "They have won me back over with this one-piece. I don’t like compression in a bikini but I love it in a one-piece.

    "This is probably the most snatching and supportive swimsuit I have ever tried on in my life. It makes me feel snatched, it makes my boobs look amazing, wow," Raeann added.
