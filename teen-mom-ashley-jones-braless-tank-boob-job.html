    However, she sported a different tune in a recent post where she aired her grievances about the production of Teen Mom 2.

    BUTTING HEADS

    Ashley posted a photo of her with her friend and business partner Jessica and their children at the grand opening of their beauty salon Aries Beauty Studio.

    The caption read: "A moment that was filmed but not shown for some reason, my GRAND opening party. You may or may not know that I’ve opened up an aesthetic salon @ariesbeautystudio with a very good friend of mine. IT'S BEEN A YEAR SINCE WE STARTED. Time flies. So grateful."

    She also added: "I was very hurt that this scene was NOT shown because all while possibly going through a divorce I was focused and persistent in my business, and THAT is a story to be shown. Nevertheless, I am grateful for them putting me in a place to be able to really grow and build."

    Teen Mom fans hurried to the post's comment section to address the cut footage.

    One fan commented: "Because Success doesn’t sell ratings, only drama. Kudos to your success."

    Another added, "This is the type of accomplishment that viewers want to see."

    While a third joked, "I’m sure they’ll show that trash party they filmed the other day though."

    'IT'S MESSY'

    Ashley turned her frustrations on Dr. Drew Pinsky in a post the day before, slamming the physician for his questions during the show's reunion special.

    "Let me make something extremely clear. I would never sit in a room and be silent while my friend is being chopped, for that purpose I removed myself," she began, referencing her co-star Kailyn Lowry's absence from the in-person taping.

    "It's not about control, it's about loyalty. Kail [Lowry] was there via Zoom, why wasn't she present for this segment. It's messy. Period," she ranted.

    In a second post, she added: "And the next time you ask me why I'm in it or making a comment refer to this video. It's always 2 against 1 and respectfully, Kail got friends too."

    REUNION DRAMA

    It all started when Dr. Drew addressed that Kailyn, who left the show midseason, was absent from the reunion stage.

    As the host continued to press the ladies on the subject, Ashley suddenly stood up and walked off the set.

    On her way out, she told Dr. Drew: "I'm going to let you all talk about Kail without me cause I don't wanna be in none of that s**t."

    Her castmate and Kailyn's former BFF, Leah Messer, quickly joined her, saying on her way out: "I agree."

    Ashley still may not be over the drama, as she revealed on Kailyn's Barely Famous podcast earlier this month that she and Bar, 25, are considering moving their family to the UK.
