    Xbox One X bundle with Fifa 19, Tomb Raider and Now TV is CHEAPER than buying the console outright

    WE'VE found an absolute smasher of an Xbox One X bundle that gets you two games and Now TV for less than the price of the console. Better still, the games included are Shadow of the Tomb Raider and the brand new Fifa 19, which only came out today.

    05 Jul 2024, 00:00

    WE'VE found an absolute smasher of an Xbox One X bundle that gets you two games and Now TV for less than the price of the console.

    Better still, the games included are Shadow of the Tomb Raider and the brand new Fifa 19, which only came out today.

    This GAME deal costs £399.99, and gets you the Xbox One X (1TB), two games, and two months of Now TV Entertainment.

    GAME itself lists the bundle's true value as £509.98, and says the saving is £109.99.

    But is that a fair assessment of the price? We think the deal is actually even better.

    Buying an Xbox One X today will cost you £449.99 from GAME – or a slightly cheaper £409.99 at Argos.

    • Xbox One X bundle at GAME for £399.99 – buy now

    Meanwhile, Fifa 19 currently costs £49.99 for Xbox One at most stores, and Shadow of the Tomb Raider costs £47.99 at Smyth's Toys.

    And Now TV will cost you £7.99 a month for the Entertainment Pass – so that's £15.98 over two months.

    That means our valuation of this bundle comes in at £523.95.

    So you're actually saving a whopping £123.96.

    Not only that, but the bundle is at least £10 cheaper than buying the Xbox One X outright.

    Microsoft launched the Xbox One X in November last year, to widespread critical acclaim.

    The big advantage of the new console is that it supports 4K gaming.

    It can also play games in HDR, or High Dynamic Range, which means you'll see improved contrast and more colours – assuming you have a compatible HDR telly.

    Lots of popular games support both 4K and HDR for the Xbox One X, including Assassin's Creed Odyssey, Fifa 19, and Halo: The Master Chief Collection.

    The Xbox One X console also features a boosted 1TB of storage, making it harder for you to run out of space for game installations.
