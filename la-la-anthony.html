    Bio:
    La La Anthony (born Alani Nicole Vazquez on June 25, 1979 in Brooklyn, New York) is an American radio and television personality, New York Times best-selling author, producer and actress. In the early 2000s, La La came into the light as a VJ on MTV’s Total Request Live. In 2003, she launched her own production company, Krossover Productions, and was a producer on the critically-acclaimed documentary, Tyson. She made her film debut in 2001 in Two Can Play That Game, but her most well-known acting roles were in the 2012 film Think Like A Man and the 2013 comedy, Baggage Claim. In October 2011, La La starred in and produced five seasons of La La’s Full Court Wedding on Vh1, which documented the time leading up to her wedding to NBA star Carmelo Anthony. Their life continued to be chronicled on the show La La’s Full Court Life. On January 28, 2014, La La released her first book titled, The Love Playbook, where she shares how she found love and success through hard work. Her second book, The Power Playbook, was released in May 2015. Most recently, La La produced the Broadway production of Eclipsed.

    Best Known For:
    La La Anthony is best known for her role in Think Like a Man and her show La La’s Full Court Wedding.

    Personal Life:
    La La married Carmelo Anthony on July 10, 2010, six years after becoming engaged. In the years they were engaged, La La gave birth to their son, Kiyan Carmelo Anthony, on March 7, 2010. Sadly, on April 17, 2017, it was revealed that La La and Carmelo had separated and were living apart. The couple haven’t announced any plans to divorce.
