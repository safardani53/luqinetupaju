    A BARGAIN hunter found an easy way to make $500 with an incredible deal from Walmart.

    He often shops at Walmart and uses the store's app to check on hidden clearance items.

    Chaz Dunn (@chazzzd) shares affordable products you can resell to make money on his TikTok.

    These are products in the store that haven't been properly marked down. In some cases, there will be no sign the item is on sale, while in other cases, it has a clearance price tag but hasn't been updated with the lower price.

    Chaz often tells his over 55,200 followers to "be on the lookout," or BOLO.

    In one of his most recent TikToks, Chaz told his followers to BOLO for a 16-jar spice rack that's been marked down to $5 from $22.

    Read more on Walmart

    This is already a great deal if you need a spice rack or want to snag one as a gift for someone else, but you can also make some money on this deal.

    Chaz showed that the spice racks are selling for at least $27 on Amazon.

    With fees, he'd make $22.95 for each rack he sells. He didn't specifically say how many racks he bought, but he said he'd make about $500 in profit.

    SELLING ON AMAZON

    Selling on Amazon with Fulfillment by Amazon (FBA) is easier than you'd think.

    Most read in Money

    Fellow TikToker BizWithJacky told her followers how she makes $10,000 monthly using the system.

    FBA is a service that allows sellers to outsource their order fulfillment directly to Amazon rather than packing and shipping them directly.

    Amazon handles all of that, as well as any returns.

    Jacky said you need to go to sell.amazon.com and sign up to become an Amazon seller.

    There are some fees to keep in mind, as well as tax implications that come with any side hustle.

    The individual Amazon seller plan costs 99 cents per sale, and those using the professional plan pay $39.99 per month no matter how many items they sell.

    This means the professional option may make more sense if you sell over 40 items per month.
