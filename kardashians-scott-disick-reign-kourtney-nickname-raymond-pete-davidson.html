    Kardashian fans are shocked after Scott Disick called his son Reign, seven, a nickname that apparently confused a new incomer to the Kar-Jenner clan.

    Kim Kardashian, 41, Tweeted on Friday that her boyfriend Pete Davidson, 28, thought Reign's real name was "Raymond" because that is what Scott refers to him as.

    Scott's various endearing nicknames for his kids apparently confused Pete for some time.

    In the latest Hulu The Kardashians episode, Scott referred to Reign repeatedly as "Raymond" in a scene where he catches up with Khloe as the seven-year-old and his sister Penelope play on a trampoline.

    As fans were confused as to why Scott consistently called out to Reign using "Raymond," Kim posted a Tweet to bring more information to light.

    Kim Tweeted: "Funny fact: Pete thought Reign's name was Raymond for the longest time because that's what Scott Disick calls him all the time."

    According to People, Scott has been known for using fun nicknames to refer to his kids.

    He allegedly calls Penelope "Poosh" and "Peesh" and Mason "Marty."

    Scott shares his three kids with his ex Kourtney Kardashian.

    In the same scene from the Hulu episode, Scott confessed to Khloe that he thinks Pete is a "great guy."

    As Khloe and Scott are discussing their heartbreak with each other, they bring up Kim's new relationship with Pete.

    Scott said: "Kim found a guy who is a great guy. Like, he's so sweet."

    Khloe agreed: "Yeah, so sweet."

    Scott continued: "He picked us up from the airport the other day and had the coffee she wanted, had a coffee for me, I didn't want to say it was the completely wrong one, he's just a great person."

    Khloe replied: "That's so nice."

    Scott said: "You can tell he cares about people's feelings. He was supposed to come over here actually, I don't know where he is."

    Khloe teased: "Oh my gosh are you and Pete besties?"

    Scott retorted: "Bunk beds, the whole thing. We have the best time together."

    Khloe commented: "I'm happy she has someone who is nice to her and caters to her."

    Scott agreed: "I think she hasn't had that in a really long time."

    Before Kim started dating Pete, she was struggling through a nasty divorce feud with her ex-husband Kanye West.

    THE REAL SCEET

    Pete and Scott have sparked a bromance over the months of him hanging out with the Kardashian clan.

    A source close to Scott dished about their friendship to E! News saying that the dad of three has officially "welcomed Pete into the family."

    The insider continued: "They have become good friends. They both have the best sense of humor and it's always fun when they are together."

    Pete's girlfriend Kim is supportive of the pair's closeness as the source also noted, the SKIMS Founder finds their friendship "cute."

    Scott and the comic have hung out on numerous occasions even while Kim was out of town.

    DADDY DISICK

    As for Scott, fans have been praising the KUWTK star for his "special bond" with his eldest son Mason and his kids.

    A Kardashian fan thread showed several resurfaced photos of Scott and Mason together through the years.

    In many of the photos, Mason is smiling ear-to-ear as he holds hand and looks proud to be next to his dad.

    Someone said: "Not a fan of Scott, but he sure loves his kids and seems to have great bonds with each of them."

    Another wrote: "He does, and they seem to adore him as well."

    A third posted: "I genuinely think Mason ADORES Scott. They’re like partners in crime. The kids definitely make Scott be the better version of himself."

    Yet a fourth chimed in: "I absolutely love Mason & Scott’s relationship. They share such a special bond!"

    Finally a fifth posted: "I noticed Mason has always looks the most comfortable around cameras when he’s with Scott."

    DADDY TIME

    The group watched TV and indulged in snacks when the Flip It Like Disick star took the snap.

    They all stared at the camera as Scott snapped the pic, each with a treat in hand.
