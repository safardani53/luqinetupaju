    A WOMAN has revealed what UK Primark shoppers can look forward to when shops reopen after visiting the store in the US.

    TikTok user Alyssa Himpson visited the Primark store in Boston and filmed a tour of what was on offer.

    While Primark remains closed in the UK until non-essential shops reopen on April 12, stores remain open in the US.

    Her video showcases a huge new Disney home line, including pink Mickey Mouse cushions and grey fleece blankets.

    There is also a range of Disney plushies available including characters such as Winnie the Pooh, Minnie Mouse and Thumper the rabbit.

    Those looking forward to a UK break this summer may want to pay a trip to Primark as the Boston store displayed a huge range of Disney suitcases perfect for weekends away.

    Aside from the new Disney goodies, Alyssa also revealed the new fashion trends in store including cute sling-back mules, handbags and plenty of beachwear.

    Her video has since gone viral as excitable shoppers ready themselves to splash some serious cash.

    Commenting on the vid, one wrote: “I can’t wait for this to open again.”

    “OMG holiday clothes” said another, while a third wrote, “I NEEEDDDD EVERYTHING.”

    Fabulous Digital has contacted Primark for a comment.
