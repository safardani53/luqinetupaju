    Last night on The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills Season 5 Episode 18, Brandi Glanville revealed a possible fling with an Amster-Boy, and much more.

    "The thought of spending the night with a hunk or five menopausal mamas … I’m picking the hunk," she said of the young man, 23, she met in Amsterdam.

    In the city’s Red Light District, obviously.

    Brandi Glanville went on a date with Andre, who as it turned out is a friend of Lisa Vanderpump’s son Max. Good cougar action across the Atlantic.

    Lisa was still upset at her, but not over events on The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills Season 5 Episode 18. She was fuming over being slapped.

    "I was joking," Glanville said, extending an olive branch (or at least tulips) to her former best friend. "I took it too far, as she has with me many times."

    Vanderpump responded that she "crossed the line one too many times. I have been her emotional punching bag, I refuse to be her physical punching bag."

    Brandi also claims that she’s never forgiven for her mistakes and antics, while things like Lisa Rinna-Kim Richards fight will quickly be glossed over.

    She makes a valid point, although there are two caveats:

  • Her antics do seem designed to cause drama, and are often full-on ridiculous in nature, rather than isolated, brief but heated exchanges.
  • Brandi Glanville doesn’t seem apologetic or try to change her ways in response. Not that she necessarily should, even. But it’s an observation.

    There’s no glossing over the fact that Kyle and Kim Richards aren’t seeing eye to eye, though, and that the siblings’ reconciliation appears bleak.

    "I’m not the same person I was three years ago, stop treating me like that," said Kim to her sister, adding that Kyle’s nemesis Brandi has had her back.

    "Guess what she said behind your back?" Kyle responded.

    "She’s saying, ‘Kim needs an intervention.’"

    They proceeded to fight over Kyle’s sale of their mother’s old Palm Springs house, with Kyle saying that Kim twists and lies about pretty much everything.

    That didn’t sit well. "You’re lying! You’re lying! I’m so done with you and your f–king lies," Kim responded as things turned south very, very fast in the 90210.
