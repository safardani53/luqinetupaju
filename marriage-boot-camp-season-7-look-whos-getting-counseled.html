    WE tv is here with our very first look at Marriage Boot Camp Reality Stars’ Season 7.

    And it looks like viewers are in for a series of crazy episodes when this reality show returns on January 6!

    For starters, consider the couples that will be screaming at each other on Season 7. It’s safe to say they come from rather divergent programs and backgrounds…

    There will be Ashley and JP Rosenbaum from The Bachelorette, as they fight over parenting approaches for their son.

    There will be Amy and Dillon King from 19 Kids and Counting, who apparently need professional assistance after just seven months of marriage.

    And let’s not forget about PreMadonna and Buck Thomas from Love and Hip Hop: Atlanta, who need to settle on a wedding date and prepare to actually spend the rest of their lives together.

    We’ll also be spending time with Gabi Victor and Victor Tarrats from Bad Girls Club. The former’s unexpected pregnancy will be the focus of their counseling.

    And, finally, Renee and Joey Gambino from Mob Wives will need to figure out how to tell Renee’s gangster dad about their romance. Talk about a juicy storyline!
