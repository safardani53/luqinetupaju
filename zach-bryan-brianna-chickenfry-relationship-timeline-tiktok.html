    ZACH Bryan and Brianna Chickenfry made their relationship public in July 2023.

    Now, fans want to know everything there is to know about the country star's new romance.

    Who is Brianna Chickenfry?

    Outside of being known as Zach's girlfriend, Brianna, whose real name is Brianna "Bri" LaPaglia, is known for her career as an influencer and podcast host.

    Alongside Grace O'Malley, Bri hosts the Barstool Sports podcast, PlanBri Uncut, which is where she gives a rundown of what it’s like navigating your life in your early 20’s

    "She’s not here to sugarcoat anything, sharing her insane drunken memories all while giving tips on relationships, friendships, partying & much more," the show's description reads.

    When Bri isn't behind the microphone, she can often be found creating content for her TikTok page, where she boasts more than 2million followers.

    Read More on Zach Bryan

    The self-described Dr. Phil of college advice was born on June 17, 1999, in Boston, Massachusetts, but now resides in New York City.

    When did Brianna Chickenfry and Zach Bryan meet?

    Before Zach and Bri were together, the latter was just another one of his fans.

    They first met briefly at the Academy of Country Music Awards in May 2023, where she asked the country star for a photo together.

    At the time, they were both in other relationships and she has since insisted that they "didn’t say anything to each other" aside from her asking for a photo, according to People.

    It wasn't until after they had broken up with their respective partners that they reconnected.

    It was then during an 18-hour car ride that Bri knew Zach was special.

    "He’s like, the nicest, most genuine person I’ve ever met. He’s so normal," she told cohosts Dave Portnoy, Josh Richards, and Grace on an episode of the BFFs podcast.

    "We did a f***ing 18-hour car ride together and … didn’t turn the music on once and talked the whole time.

    "That’s when I knew, like, oh this guy’s actually awesome. We got to know each other. We’ve been spending a lot of time together," she continued.

    On July 28, 2023, she then confirmed their relationship on her podcast.

    Has Zach Bryan been married?

    When Zach first met Bri, he was in a relationship with Deb Peifer, but prior to that, he was married to Rose Madden.

    Zach and Rose tied the knot in 2020 but divorced the following year for an unknown reason.

    The former pair reportedly met while serving in the military.
