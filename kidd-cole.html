    Bio:
    Kidd Cole (born Jerez Nehemiah Stone-Coleman on January 17, 1995) is an American rapper. Though claiming to be a hip-hop producer, Kidd gained his most fame from appearing on a May 2014 episode of the MTV television series, Catfish. During the episode, Kidd said he was a part of Kanye West’s GOOD Music record label. After he was revealed as a fraud, he admitted he grew up in foster care and that his scam was an attempt to get attention. Kidd refused to do a follow-up interview. Kidd was revealed to be awaiting mental evaluation after he was caught scamming Virginia Commonwealth University into thinking he was arranging an appearance by Big Sean. Kidd is reportedly a producer who worked with West Coast rapper YG. In May 2015, Kidd was arrested for making 11 bomb and terror threats against Washington, D.C. Metro trains and busses.

    Best Known For:
    Kidd Cole is best known for his appearance on MTV’s Catfish and for being arrested for making terror threats.

    Personal Life:  
    Weeks after Kidd’s Catfish aired, R&B Divas star Lil’ Mo posted on Instagram that she used to be married to Kidd’s father, Gus Stone. Lil’ Mo revealed that Cole has two half-sisters, named Heaven and God’Iss Love. She revealed that Cole grew up with his grandmother and never saw his father while she and Gus were together.
