    For the first time in all of Super Bowl history, a pair of brothers, Jason Kelce and Travis Kelce, faced off at the Super Bowl in 2023. Travis Kelce is a tight end for the Chiefs and older brother Jason is a starting center for the Eagles.

    The boys were drafted in 2011 and 2013, and ever since dad and mom Ed and Donna Kelce have been jetting around the country to support their sons. Donna even made news this season when she was able to catch games in both Tampa Bay and Kansas City on the same day in Jan. 2023, as reported by Yahoo Sports. Though rivals on the field, brothers Travis and Jason Kelce have always had each other’s backs and even have a podcast together called ‘Great Heights,’ in which they talk about their careers and personal lives.
