    Francesco Clemente Giuseppe Sparanero, known as Franco Nero, is an Italian actor. His breakout role was the main character in Sergio Corbucci's Spaghetti Western Django, a role he re-enacted in Nello Rossati's Django Strikes Again. In 2012, Nero appeared as a cameo in Quentin Tarantino's Django Unchained. Since then he has performed over 200 leading and supporting roles in numerous films and TV shows in Italy and abroad, in genres ranging from poliziotteschi, action, theater, war and musical comedies. These include the Bible: In the Beginning ..., Camelot, The Day of the Owl, The Mercenary, The Battle of Neretva, Tristana, Compañeros, The Confession of a Police Captain, High Crime, Law of the Road Street, Keoma, Hitch- Hike, Navarone Force 10, Enter the Ninja, Die Hard 2, Megiddo: The Omega Code 2, Letters to Juliet and John Wick: Chapter 2 .
